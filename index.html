HOME AUTOMATION

With a single button, you can make the entire house go to sleep; it will lock all the doors, shut your curtains, adjust the home temperature, and set your alarm clock and many more at your fingertips. Home automation system makes life inside most convenient for houses of every size. The control system works in the background and does the job for you without causing any mishap to the interior décor of the place. Adjustable dimmer switches, smart thermostats, automated blinds and other features will then be included in the design along with the automation system itself.

SOFT FURNISHING

Soft furnishing is always a great way to add a luxurious and polished finish to your room. With a variety of textures, prints, patterns, designs and fabrics, soft furnishing leads to dressing up your room as per your choices. During different times of the year, season and just how you use your house may change with the season.Be it a warm and comfortable feel, majestic and rich, minimalistic and many more, soft furnishing is readily alterable. The ability to rapidly shift décor things that are soft enables a substantial transformation of an inside with minimal effort.
